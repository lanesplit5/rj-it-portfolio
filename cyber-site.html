Common Online Threats

01

Phishing

Fake emails or messages can trick users into giving away passwords, payment details, or personal information.

02

Weak Passwords

Short or reused passwords make it easier for attackers to break into accounts.

03

Public WiFi Risks

Unsecured networks can expose logins, browsing activity, and sensitive information.

Safety Tips

  • Use unique passwords for important accounts.
  • Turn on multi-factor authentication when available.
  • Verify links before clicking them.
  • Avoid entering private information on public WiFi.
