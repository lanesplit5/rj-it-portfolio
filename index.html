Cybersecurity Awareness Website

This is a working mini website created to teach users how to protect their personal information online. It explains common risks in a clear way so everyday users can understand what to avoid.

  • Purpose: Help users recognize threats like phishing, weak passwords, and unsafe public WiFi.
  • Audience: Students and general internet users.
  • Front end: Landing section, threat cards, safety tips, and clean navigation.
  • Back end thinking: Organized content structure that makes information easy to scan and use.

Excel Dashboard System

This visual represents an Excel dashboard system used to organize data, track activity, and show performance trends. The goal is to make information easier to understand without forcing users to sort through raw data.

  • Purpose: Track totals, compare months, and summarize performance.
  • Audience: Supervisors, team leads, and staff members.
  • Front end: Charts, KPI cards, and summary visuals.
  • Back end thinking: Excel formulas, structured data tables, and clean categories.

Social Media Strategy

Building a stronger online presence.

Using ideas from Forbes' Social Media Marketing: The Ultimate Guide, I would build a social media presence by setting clear goals, understanding the target audience, creating useful content, staying consistent, engaging with the community, and measuring performance.

  • Define goals such as awareness, engagement, and conversions.
  • Understand the audience and the platforms they use most.
  • Create content that informs, connects, and supports the brand.
  • Track results and adjust based on what performs best.
